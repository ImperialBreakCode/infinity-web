3D Lightsaber

This is a 3D model of a lightsaber that you can interact with. You can ignite, retract the blade, change its color and more. Solaris lightsaber is still in early development so bugs can be expected. Design changes and additions such as more sound effects are planned for the future - more colors, more hilts, blade effects and lightsaber movements.

Help

Control Buttons
  • toggle blade - turns the saber on/off
  • lockup - simulates saber lockup
Alternatives and more controls
  • press 's' to ignite/retract the blade
  • press and hold 'c' to simulate blade lockup
  • left click to rotate the view
  • right click to move the camera
  • use the scroll to zoom in or out
